What is Big O??
-->Big O tells us how fast or slow an algorithm works as the amount of data increases
it measures how much work the computer must do 

There are two 2 types of search operations:-
1.Linear search-->this algorithm checks all the element one by one
2.Binary search-->it doesnt check one by one rather it assigns low and high calculates mid value and then find the vaalues according to high and low

but for both these search operation the array need to be sorted
